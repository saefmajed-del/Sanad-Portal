## ⚡ Quick Tips for Use

### Best models for each:
- **Hero booth shots (1, 5, 7):** Gemini Imagen 3 (Google AI Studio) — best at corporate/governmental aesthetic
- **Robot close-ups (2, 3, 4):** Midjourney v6 — best at material rendering + dramatic lighting
- **Top-down diagrams (6):** DALL-E 3 — best at isometric/technical illustration
- **Detail/macro (8):** Midjourney v6 — best at product-shot lighting

### For Arabic text in images:
**Don't generate it in the image** — image models will hallucinate broken Arabic. Either:
1. Tell the model "Arabic text not critical, can be stylized/blurred"
2. Add text in post via Photoshop, Canva, or Figma (use IBM Plex Sans Arabic + Noto Naskh Arabic — fonts already in the project)

### Speed pass:
لو وقتك ضيق، اعمل **PROMPT 1 و PROMPT 5** بس — يعطيك hero booth + visitor lifestyle. كافي لاجتماع بكره.

### Color codes (للتعديل في Photoshop):
- Deep navy: `#0A1428`
- Warm gold: `#C4A572`
- Gold highlight: `#D4B785`
- Cream paper: `#FBF9F4`
- Saudi green: `#00A86B`
